Ideas for backsplash layout

  1. Single socket left of the cooktop — blender and small appliances.
  2. Two-gang block at the prep zone — mixer + phone charger.
  3. Socket + switch in one panel above the sink (outside splash zone).
  4. Horizontal row of 3 gangs behind the coffee station.
  5. Low socket near the oven for built-in appliances.
  6. Block at island side — if stoneware continues on the peninsula.
  7. Hidden switch for under-cabinet lighting in the same plane as sockets.
  8. Symmetrical pairs on both sides of the hood niche.
  9. Extra gang for water filter or smart tap power supply.
